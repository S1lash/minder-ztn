# Long-form playbook — how to build a long-form deliverable for you

> Your recipe for **standalone artifacts consumed linearly** — a report,
> longread, audiobook, debrief — as opposed to a quick chat answer. Loaded
> **on demand**, only when such a deliverable is actually requested (trigger in
> `communication-baseline → Long-form deliverables`). Never pulled for ordinary
> chat answers, so long-form work doesn't drag your whole identity into context.
>
> The universal spine (conclusion-first, structure, no fluff, no sycophancy)
> lives in `communication-baseline`; your personal presentation deltas live in
> your `ai-interaction` principles. Below is only what is specific to YOUR
> long-form.

> e.g. "Lead every section with why it matters. Roughly half meaning, a third
> facts, the rest story. A new layer every few minutes. End each part with the
> rule we just learned. Ban filler and purple prose."
