# What counts (biometric-cross-domain)

- Cross-source bridges with effect_size ≥ 0.2 in Tier II Phase 2
  (biometric × affect) — anxious → next-day HRV drop, conflict
  → next-night sleep loss, etc.
- Phase 1 cross-source findings where one side is meta-derived
  (meeting_heavy_day → sleep_h) — i.e. structural-tag-on-numeric
  analyses.
- Pattern repeating ≥ 5 days in the window with consistent direction.
- Counter-evidence: dates where the pattern fails. Mandatory in every
  hypothesis.
