# What doesn't count (biometric-life-synthesis)

- Synthesis without primary-record citations — lens output alone is
  hypothesis-grade, never the basis for a claim. Anchoring constraint
  binds every claim to primary owner-data.
- Single-day claims — that's `biometric-anomaly-narrator`.
- Within-biometric findings without life-narrative bridge — those
  belong to `biometric-cross-domain`.
- Recommendations / advice. The lens surfaces; owner decides.
- Coaching tone («you should…»). Narrative tone — describing what
  was observed.
- Causation language. Association only.
