# What counts (biometric-life-synthesis)

- Multi-source bridge claims grounded in cited primary records.
- Convergence between life narrative (journal, SOUL focus) and
  biometric pattern (Tier II findings).
- Divergence — stated calmness vs lived stress markers, declared
  health goal vs adherence, planned focus vs where attention went.
- Falsifiable suggested experiment for next week.
- Strong-tier finding (n ≥ 10, effect ≥ 0.5) triggers Memory note
  section — title, observation, decision, review date.
